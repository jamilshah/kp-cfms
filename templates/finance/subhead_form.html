What are Sub-Heads?

Sub-Heads provide optional subdivisions of NAM Heads (Level 4) for more granular internal tracking.

  • Example: A01101 (Basic Pay - Officers) can have sub-heads:
    • A01101-01: Grade 17-22 Officers
    • A01101-02: Grade 11-16 Officers
  • Effect: When you create sub-heads, the parent NAM Head becomes a rollup-only head (no direct posting)
